Player 1 Controls

Move Left:  A key
Move Right: D key
Jump:       W key
Attack 1:   LShift key


Player 2 Controls

Move Left:  Left Arrow key
Move Right: Right Arrow key
Jump:       Up Arrow Key
Attack 1:   RShift key


Eliminate the opponent player by dealing damage to their health bar with your attacks.
The player that gets their opponent's health bar to 0 wins the game.
